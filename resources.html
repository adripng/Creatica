How To Help

Some ways to help with preventing the extinction of these species are to learn about these species and spread awareness about them. The following are resources that can help you start on this journey and references that we used. Avoid buying products that are made using threatened or endangered species, and contribute to protecting wildlife habitats. The destruction of habitats is the biggest threat these animals face today. We live in an industrial world, there's a lot of logging, oil and gas drilling, and so on, which destroys the natural wilderness. Put your best effort forward to reduce pollution and preserve these environments.
